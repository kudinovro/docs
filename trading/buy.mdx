---
title: "Buy via Link & Keyword"
description: "Find and trade any Polymarket market by sending a link or keyword directly in the chat"
---

You don't need to navigate through any menu to find a market. Just type directly in the Tyche chat.

## Buy via keyword

Type any word or topic into the chat — Tyche will search Polymarket and return a list of matching markets.

**Examples:**
- `bitcoin`
- `fed rate`
- `election`
- `trump`

Select a market from the results to open its detail view and place a trade.

## Buy via link

Paste any Polymarket market URL directly into the chat. Tyche instantly fetches the market and opens its detail view — no searching needed.

**Example:**
```
https://polymarket.com/event/some-market-slug
```

## Market detail view

Once a market is opened (via keyword or link), you'll see:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| YES price / NO price | Current market prices |
| Best bid / Best ask | Top of order book |
| Liquidity | Available liquidity in USD |
| Volume | Total market volume |

From here, tap **Buy YES**, **Buy NO**, or **Set Limit Order**.

## Confirm order

After selecting a side:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| Market | The question you are betting on |
| Side | YES or NO |
| Amount | From your active preset |

| Button | Action |
|---|---|
| Confirm | Place the order at market price |
| Change Amount | Override the amount for this trade |
| Change Preset | Switch to a different preset |
| Cancel | Abort without placing the order |

<Tip>
  Set up a [Preset](/features/presets) with your preferred amount so you can confirm trades with a single tap.
</Tip>
