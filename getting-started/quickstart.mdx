## 1. Open the bot

Open [@Tyche_Polymarket_bot](https://t.me/Tyche_Polymarket_bot) in Telegram and press **Start**.

You'll see the welcome screen with your auto-generated Polymarket wallet address and the main menu.

The welcome screen also includes quick resource links:

| Resource | Description |
|---|---|
| Tyche Docs | Open the documentation |
| Tyche X | Follow Tyche on [X](https://x.com/TycheTerminal) |
| Tyche Community | Join the Telegram community |

## 2. Your wallet

Tyche automatically creates a Polymarket wallet for you (W1). You can see:

- **Wallet address** — your Polygon address on Polymarket
- **pUSD Balance** — funds available to trade on Polymarket
- **Open Positions** — number of active bets
- **Positions Value** — current market value of your positions
- **Total Balance** — pUSD Balance + Positions Value

<Note>
  Your wallet is self-custodial. Tyche never holds your funds — they live on the Polymarket smart contracts. You can export your private key at any time.
</Note>

## 3. Deposit funds

Tap **Wallet** → **Deposit** and choose your network. The minimum deposit is **$2.00**.

All deposits are credited as **pUSD** on Polymarket.

Supported networks: Polygon, Ethereum, Base, Arbitrum, Solana
