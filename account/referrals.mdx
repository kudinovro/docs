Tap **Referrals** from the main menu to access your referral link and track your earnings.

Earn **30%** from your referrals' fees. Share your link and rewards are added automatically.

## Referral link

Your referral link is shown at the top of the page.

Example:

```
https://t.me/Tyche_Polymarket_bot?start=ref_...
```

Send this link to anyone you want to invite. When they start the bot through your link, they are connected to your referral account.

## Stats

The referral page shows:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| Invited | Total users who joined through your link |
| Active referrals | Referrals who have active trading activity |
| Referral volume | Total trading volume generated by your referrals |
| Pending rewards | Rewards earned but not yet paid out |
| Paid out | Rewards already paid |
| Total earned | Pending rewards + paid out rewards |

Use **Back** to return to the previous screen, or **Home** to return to the main menu.
