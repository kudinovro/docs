title: "Trending"
description: "Browse the hottest Polymarket markets by volume and trade instantly"
---

Tap **Trending** from the main menu to see the top markets on Polymarket right now.

## Market feed

The feed shows up to 60 markets, paginated 10 per page (Page 1/6).

Each market entry shows:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| Market name | The prediction market question |
| YES price | Current price to buy YES (in cents) |
| NO price | Current price to buy NO (in cents) |
| Volume | Total traded volume in USD |
| Buy | Tap to open the market and place an order |

Use **Next** to go to the next page, and **Refresh** to reload the latest data.

## Buying from Trending

Tap **Buy** on any market to open its detail view:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| YES price / NO price | Current market prices |
| Best bid / Best ask | Order book top of book |
| Liquidity | Available liquidity in USD |
| Volume | Total market volume in USD |
| Condition | On-chain market condition ID |

From here, tap **Buy YES**, **Buy NO**, or **Set Limit Order**.

### Market order

After selecting a side, a **Confirm order** screen appears:

- **Market** — the question you are betting on
- **Side** — YES or NO
- **Amount** — the dollar amount (from your active preset or last used amount)

From the confirm screen you can:

| Button | Action |
|---|---|
| Confirm | Place the order immediately |
| Change Amount | Enter a different dollar amount |
| Change Preset | Switch to a different saved preset |
| Cancel | Abort the order |

<Tip>
  Set up a [Preset](/features/presets) with your preferred amount and risk settings to place orders from Trending with a single tap.
</Tip>

### Limit order

Tap **Set Limit Order** to place an order at a specific price instead of the current market price.
