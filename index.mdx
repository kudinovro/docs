## What is Tyche?

Tyche is a Telegram bot ([@Tyche_Polymarket_bot](https://t.me/Tyche_Polymarket_bot)) for trading on [Polymarket](https://polymarket.com) — the world's largest prediction market platform. Trade directly from your Telegram chat, track your portfolio, copy top traders, and automate your strategy.
